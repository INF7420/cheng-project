Why CTK & Co.

Doing business with CTK & Co. is choosing the personal touch and not being regarded as just another client or policy number. Our small team of senior brokers blend years of experience in our sector, a deep knowledge of the Asian market, and the freshness and vigor of a youthful company.

We believe in close working relationships and providing a truly bespoke insurance service for our clients. We keep things personal by providing a dedicated point of contact - a senior broker who will take full responsibility for your business.

We offer:

Experience

We have a unique insight into the business culture of the Asia Pacific region, and longstanding relationships with all the underwriting markets.

Enthusiasm

No risk is deemed too small or too complex for our team. We're forward thinking, innovative, and will always strive to find the best solution for your needs.

Service

We set ourselves high standards when it comes to response times, and understand the job isn't over once a risk has been placed. Integrity
