Trevel

Travel Insurance plans to ensure that you are protected against a wide and diverse array of risks while outside of Hong Kong. With options for plans which only provide medical insurance, and for policies which provide extended umbrella protection for a range of coverage benefits, a travel insurance policy can help to provide peace-of-mind that you will have the assistance you require should anything go wrong while you are overseas.

Key Features

* Medical Insurance

* Personal Accident Insurance

* Cancellation of Flights and Delay of Journey

* Loss of Baggage and Personal Possessions

* Personal Liability Insurance

* Trip Cancellation
